Easy Breakfast Tortilla Pizza



Description

This is a breakfast tortilla pizza recipe.

Breakfast tortilla pizza is a quick and easy breakfast that everyone will love. Just four basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 tsp Salt and Pepper
  • 3 egg
  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degress F
  2. Rub butter along the bottom and sides of a 8 or 9 inch cake pan. Press tortilla down into the buttered pan and against the sides. Crack each egg next to each other on the tortilla. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and add desired toppings evenly on top.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are cooked to your liking, about 13-15 minutes
